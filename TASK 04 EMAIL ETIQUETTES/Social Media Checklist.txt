Social Media Checklist 


DO'S:


Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Always use an extra layer of security beyond just a password to protect your accounts from unauthorized access.

Review Privacy Settings: Regularly check who can see your posts and personal information on platforms like Instagram and Facebook.

Think Before You Post: Remember that once something is online, it is permanent; ensure your content reflects a professional image for future recruiters.

Verify Information: Always fact-check news or academic information before resharing it to avoid spreading digital misinformation.

Network Professionally: Use platforms like LinkedIn to follow industry leaders and alumni in the AI and Machine Learning field.

DON'TS:

Don't Share Location in Real-Time: Avoid "checking in" or posting your exact current location to protect your physical safety.

Don't Post Sensitive Data: Never share your registration number, home address, or phone number in public posts or stories.

Don't Engage with Trolls: Avoid arguing with anonymous accounts or cyberbullies, as this can escalate and damage your digital reputation.

Don't Use Weak Passwords: Never use easily guessable passwords like "123456" or your name; use a mix of symbols and numbers instead.

Don't Plagiarize Content: Never claim someone else’s code, digital art, or research as your own when sharing on social platforms.
